Seasonal Rithucharyas

For Those Who Love Nature and Follow the Seasons

SMAA is a private wellness club for individuals who are ready to align their lives with the rhythms of nature through Ayurvedic seasonal routines.

We guide and support people who are willing to follow Rithucharyas as detailed in the Ayur Vedam. These routines are not for everyone—they are for those who embrace the seasons, respect natural rhythms, and wish to nourish themselves with purpose.

I. Understanding the Seasons

  • Shishira (Winter): Mid-January to Mid-March
  • Vasanta (Spring): Mid-March to Mid-May
  • Grishma (Summer): Mid-May to Mid-July
  • Varsha (Monsoon): Mid-July to Mid-September
  • Sharad (Autumn): Mid-September to Mid-November
  • Hemanta (Pre-Winter): Mid-November to Mid-January

II. Diet

  • Eat seasonal foods that are locally available and fresh.
  • Adjust food qualities to balance the dominant doshas of each season.

III. Lifestyle

  • Modify daily routines such as sleep, exercise, and relaxation according to the season.
  • Practice self-care activities that promote physical and mental well-being, including meditation and music.

IV. Herbal Support

  • Use seasonal herbs and aromas that balance doshas and support overall health.

V. Cleansing Treatments

  • Practice seasonal cleansing and rejuvenation treatments to maintain health and vitality.

By adapting your lifestyle, diet, skincare, and wellness rituals to the changing seasons, you can unlock your natural beauty and radiance. SMAA Beauty Club will support you throughout this journey with customized products and services.
